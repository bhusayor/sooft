About

Beyond pretty screens

I design where complexity meets people — medical onboarding, AI workflows, food decisions, financial trust. My job is translating that complexity into calm, confident interfaces.

I ship. I work directly with founders, engineers and data teams across onboarding, dashboards, growth loops and brand tone — not in isolation, but as one story. I prototype in code when it gets the point across faster.

I measure my work in real behaviour change: healthier eating, safer check-ins, faster briefs, successful sign-ups. Based in Lagos, designing for the world.
