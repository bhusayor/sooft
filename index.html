About James

A man defined by purpose and generosity

James Okafor grew up understanding firsthand the power of community — how a single act of kindness can ripple outward and transform lives. Today, he dedicates his time, resources, and influence to causes that matter most to the people who need it most.

From feeding the homeless on cold Lagos mornings, to mentoring youth in underserved communities, James brings warmth and intentionality to every initiative he leads.

He is a graduate of the University of Lagos, a proud father, and an unwavering believer in the potential of ordinary people to do extraordinary things.

What He Stands For

Causes close to his heart

01
Feeding the Homeless
Every weekend, James personally organizes hot meal drives across local communities — ensuring that no one goes to bed hungry in his city.
02
Youth Scholarship Fund
James has funded over 120 scholarships for brilliant but underprivileged secondary school and university students since 2019.
03
Clean Water Initiative
Through partnerships with local NGOs, James has helped provide clean, reliable water access to 3 underserved communities in Ogun State.
His Journey

Milestones that shaped the man

2015
First Community Kitchen
Launched a modest Friday meal drive with 3 volunteers. Within 6 months, the program grew to serve 200 families weekly.
2018
Okafor Foundation Established
Formally registered as a non-profit, enabling larger funding partnerships and structured youth programs.
2021
State Humanitarian Award
Recognized by the Lagos State government for outstanding contribution to community welfare and youth development.
2024
5,000 Lives Milestone
Crossed a landmark — over five thousand individuals directly impacted through food, education, and water programs.
